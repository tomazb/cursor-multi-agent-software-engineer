# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive project environment. Participation should make the project safer and more useful for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate and specific in technical discussion.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while still addressing impact.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and confidential information.
- Accept correction and make space for different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption, and disclosure of private or security-sensitive information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner.
